About me


Hello world!

When trying to solve problems, I like to look at them from various perspectives.
Some people criticize this as being inefficient, and indeed, in terms of producing results quickly, this way of solving problems is of course inefficient.
Sometimes my approach takes a lot of time, and this can have drawbacks at times.
However, I believe this approach has a creative component, especially in the academic perspective.

Above all, to think from various perspectives is, for me, more interesting and enjoyable.
When trying to truly understand a problem or a question, I believe tackling that question from many angles is the best way.
This perspective has helped me to develop my academic creativity, and has also firmly established my background in computational algorithms.
